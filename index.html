About

Dr. Avinash Pujari is a Consultant Surgical Oncologist with advanced training in complex cancer surgery and a strong interest in the integration of technology and artificial intelligence into oncologic care.

He completed his MCh in Surgical Oncology from Tata Memorial Hospital, Mumbai—one of Asia's leading cancer centers—and earned a Gold Medal during his MS General Surgery training at Goa Medical College. He has also undergone advanced international training in uniportal VATS at Shanghai Pulmonary Hospital.

His clinical work focuses on evidence-based, multidisciplinary cancer management with an emphasis on precision surgery, patient safety, and long-term outcomes. He performs a wide range of oncologic procedures including cytoreductive surgery with HIPEC, complex gastrointestinal and gynecologic oncology cases, and advanced laparoscopic techniques.

Beyond clinical practice, Dr. Pujari is actively exploring AI-assisted surgical planning, clinical decision support, and workflow optimization in cancer care. He is committed to systematic approaches to surgery, creating detailed surgical protocols, and sharing knowledge with the broader medical community.

Vision: Building accessible, world-class cancer care through clinical excellence and technology.
