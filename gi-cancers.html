Gastrointestinal (GI) Cancers

Cancers of the esophagus, stomach, pancreas, liver, gallbladder, colon and rectum.

When to consult a surgical oncologist

Difficulty swallowing, blood in stools, unexplained weight loss, jaundice, persistent vomiting, bowel obstruction symptoms, or a biopsy/imaging report suggesting cancer.

Typical evaluation

Endoscopy or colonoscopy with biopsy, CT scan, MRI or PET scan when needed, and multidisciplinary treatment planning.

Bring these to consultation

All reports, scan CDs, discharge summaries and current medications.
